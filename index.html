Motorsport

I can't recall a moment of my life when I didn't like sports cars.

I would always go on vacation to the French Riviera, which allowed me to go to Monaco and take pictures of nice cars with my dad's blackberry. Growing up, I would do more car spotting (especially during the annual Top Marques in Monaco) and follow the news on supercar and hypercar companies.

On top of that, I also obviously spend Formula 1 race weekends looking at cars going fast around sick racetracks.

Music

I grew up listening to David Guetta and Tokio Hotel. Bizarre mix, I know.

But things got serious when I had to stop doing sports due to a medical condition. I started playing the guitar at the age of 13 and quickly became passionate about it. Nirvana, Sum 41, Metallica, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Ghost, Avenged Sevenfold... I wanted to play it all. My teacher also introduced me to jazz music to get better technique and improvisation. I did play live for small crowds a couple times, but not enough times if you ask me.

Forward to 2021, after starting my BBA at ESSEC, I met people of the DJ association of the school, which brought me back to my childhood and appreciation for David Guetta and electronic music. I learned how to DJ in nightclubs with professional equipment, and how to choose amongst genres depending on the hour and the crowd. Nowadays I really do listen to a lot of different genres from various countries. From French gypsy jazz to Polish rap and American heavy metal to Mexican raeggaeton, I always seek for new sounds with different atmospheres.

I spend a lot of time listening to music, live or not. I also love to break down songs I know, and sometimes I even make music myself, whether it is with my guitar or my laptop.

Watches

In recent years, I have learned about the prestigious watchmaking industry.

I became fascinated with the beauty and prestige of certain timepieces and had the opportunity to see with my own eyes some of the finest and rarest timepieces in the world.

I often check market prices on certain watch models, and I aspire to make my own watch collection in the foreseeable future.
